Frequently Asked Questions

What level should I take?

We suggest starting at a lower level just to become acquainted with the teacher and style.

What do I wear?

There is no dress code at EDGE, just wear what you feel comfortable dancing in! No ballet shoes? No problem-- feel free to rock socks for a couple classes. Sneakers are suggested for hip hop and jazz funk classes.

What's the difference between basic and beginning?

Never taken dance before? Haven't danced for a while? Basic is where to start! Basic classes will be our most beginning classes, no experience necessary! Beginning is the next level to take after you've mastered basic.

Where can I park?

We have free parking with validation for as long as your taking class at EDGE. You took class from 10am to 10pm? Ain't no thang--we got you covered. You heard right. Free parking in LA.
